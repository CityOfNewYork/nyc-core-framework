Breakpoint Modifiers

Notice the Modifier column in the table above. Breakpoint modifiers are available for many of our component and utility classes. They are useful for applying styles at a specific breakpoint (or veiwport size).

Breakpoint modifiers are always affixed to the end of the class. They include a double-dash and two-letter abbreviation representing a specific breakpoint.

Consider the border utilities, where adding .border--md to a selector applies a border at the medium breakpoint.
